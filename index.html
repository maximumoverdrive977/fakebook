What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers impersonate legitimate services or websites to trick you into providing sensitive information like usernames, passwords, or credit card numbers.

Always ensure that the website you are on is authentic by checking the URL carefully and looking for the padlock symbol in the browser's address bar. Don't be tricked into entering your information on suspicious sites!
